Hello!



My name is IL Gue Kang.
I am currently serving as a Board Director at the Sejong Research Institute.
I received my Ph.D. in Public Administration from National Chengchi University in Taiwan.
Previously, I worked as an Assistant Professor at Hansung University, and I also served as a Senior Research Fellow and Acting President at the Korea Research Institute for Vocational Education & Training(KRIVET).
My research interests include human resource development, education policy, China studies, public administration, and policy studies.
